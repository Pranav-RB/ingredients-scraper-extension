Welcome to the Skin Scraper Extension!

Your go-to tool for analyzing skincare products and finding the right solutions for your skin.

Features

  • Get personalized skincare recommendations.
  • Analyze product ingredients for compatibility.
  • Track your skin type and condition.
  • Easy to use with just a click!

How to Use

  1. Click on the extension icon in your browser.
  2. Follow the prompts to analyze your skincare products.
  3. Get recommendations tailored to your skin type!
